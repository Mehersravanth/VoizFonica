Prepaid

A prepaid subscription is where an account has to be credited with a sum of money before the service can be used. This can happen at anytime.

Postpaid

A post paid subscription is where the service is paid for after the service is delivered. For example via an automatic monthly charge to a bank account.

Dongle

A dongle is a small piece of computer hardware that connects to a port on another device to provide it with additional functionality, or enable a pass-through to such a device that adds functionality.
